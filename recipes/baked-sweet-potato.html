Baked Sweet Potato

Description

Nutritious and delicious baked sweet potato perfect for any family!

Ingredients

  • 4 medium sweet potatoes
  • salt
  • butter
  • brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Wash sweet potatoes
  3. Poke holes with fork to allow internal ventilation
  4. Bake for 1 hour or until desired tenderness
  5. Remove from oven, cut the potato skin across the top
  6. Add butter and other ingredients as toppings, enjoy!
