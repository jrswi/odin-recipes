Cheeseburger

Description

Juicy grilled chicken thighs that are delicious and easy to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 2lbs chicken thighs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • balsamic vinegar
  • lemon juice

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients into a bowl and mix
  2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat
  3. Place on grill for 8 minutes per side, until internal temp reaches minimum of 165 degrees
  4. Let meat rest for 5 minutes
