Cheeseburger

Description

The perfect cheesburger to enjoy on your weekend cookout

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • bacon
  • american cheese

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients into a bowl and mix
  2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat
  3. Place on grill for 8 minutes per side, until internal temp reaches minimum of 165 degrees
  4. Add cheese to top and let melt
  5. Add any additional preferred toppings and enjoy!
